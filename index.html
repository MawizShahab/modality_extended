    Edit Values

    As a transgender coach and entrepreneur, my core values drive my approach and interactions. Drawing from personal experiences, I am committed to supporting and empowering individuals on their journeys of self-discovery, self-acceptance, and personal growth. I also help them achieve business goals, develop leadership skills, and foster an innovative and growth-oriented mindset.

    Self-Acceptance

    Supporting individuals in embarcing their authentic selves, promoting personal growth, self-discovery, self-acceptance, and fostering a sense of empowerment in their gender identity journey.

    Leadership and Management

    Supporting entrepreneurs in enhancing their leadership and management skills, including team building, decision making, conflict resolution, and fostering a positive work culture.

    Communication

    Helping individuals develop effective Communication skills to navigate conversations about gender identity with family, friends, colleagues, and healthcare professionals.
